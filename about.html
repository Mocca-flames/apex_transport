Our Story

From Local Mover to
Pan-African Operator

Apex Transport Group has grown exponentially from a domestic heavy-commodity mover into a fully-fledged pan-African logistics operator. We specialise in end-to-end movement of bulk and heavy cargo across Southern and Central Africa.

Our model is built on one simple principle: every load must move — no excuses. Whether a shipment originates at a South African port or a remote Congolese mine, Apex takes ownership of the entire journey.
