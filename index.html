Consolidation

One Truck, Everyone's Cargo.

Instead of booking an entire truck, your cargo joins other shipments heading the same way. You pay only for the space and weight you use.

What is Consolidation?

Your cargo joins other consignments heading the same direction. One truck, multiple shippers, split cost. You are not paying for the whole vehicle — just your portion of the deck.

How It Works

Drop your freight at the depot. We match it to a truck already running your corridor. Your portion gets its own reference, its own tracking, and a delivery confirmation — the same as a dedicated load.
